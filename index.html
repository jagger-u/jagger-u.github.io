If you are a community organization

You need to create an account where we will need to verify your jurisdiction. The simplest but not the best way is to provide us with a @gov email when signing up.

There are various information you can provide: images, comments, and most importantly location addresses. You will be able to select areas with our interface. With these data, we will be able to calculate the risk for anyone concerned about a particular location.

Community organizations like you have the greatest impact on this platform as you provide the necessary reliable information about suspect groups. We rely on the data you provide.
